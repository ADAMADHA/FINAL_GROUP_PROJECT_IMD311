Brief Information About Our Company

Dash Bilis is the name of our company whereby we are known as the food company which was establish in an effort to tackle the demand of people in our society as well as evolved in the food industry business. In this context, we sell and produced instant food snacks notorious as “Bilis Rangup

Generally, Dash Bilis is a business that was establish whereby it focuses on the demand and needs in food industry. In this context, our company produced and sell a product of crispy anchovies notorious as “Bilis Rangup” which was made with a special ingredient that has never been seen in an effort to provide a variety of tastes towards the customer. For instances, we sell a premium anchovies with a special flavour such as Korean spicy, salted egg, seaweed, BBQ and original flavour.

During this post-independence era, the needs and demand towards instant food is huge. This is well to be said because as we are living in the post-independence era nowadays, it is undeniable to be said that the demand of the instant food is rapidly increasing among the inhabitants due to the affordable prices and as well as the easy-to-eat criteria. In fact, instant food serving is a food that can be eaten immediately without involving any cooking methods. Thus, we tried to tackle the demand and the needs of our society nowadays by producing our owned instant food known as “Bilis Rangup” whereby it is a crispy anchovies which was made with a special ingredient that has never been seen. To illustrate, product of Dash Bilis is using the main ingredient of anchovies that come out with three appetizing flavours of Korean spicy, salted egg, seaweed, BBQ and original flavour. Thus, that is our main reason to produce and sell this product whereby it is clearly to be seen as a product that will evolved and fulfil all of the demand and the need of our society nowadays by providing a appetizing instant food with an owned special ingredient
