Detail Information Regarding Our Product

The key activities for our company are to produce and sell DASH Bilis products that have various flavors such as Korean spicy, salted egg, seaweed, BBQ and original flavor. We produce our products in terms of materials, manufacturing, packaging and marketing to customers. This business aims to increase instant food that uses anchovies. As we know, the use of anchovies is usually only known as a side dish of traditional food which is "Nasi lemak". Thereby, we believe that we can change the perspective of our society by producing an instant food with our own special twist known as Dash Bilis which will provide a variety of appetizing flavor of anchovies. We market this product more widely so that this product is known by many people and at the same time can attract customers to buy our product. It also aims to provide simple and delicious meals to consumers.

For your information, what makes our product unique is that many people make instant food, but no one makes anchovies as instant food that can be used as a snack as well as daily food. This is because most people make it as anchovy sauce. In addition, the packaging is also made by our own company. Most of the instant food uses other parties to make their packaging but our company takes the opportunity to make our own food packaging so that our instant food can be maintained with good quality and packaging. Bilis Rangup has a competitive edge in that it provides clients with natural ingredients and seasonal flavors based on fresh ingredients. We make our own packaging to make sure that the packaging is safe without any prohibited substances that can cause stale fast food. Furthermore, we need to make sure that the packaging is safe so that the food lasts longer and also the packaging is not easily approached by insects such as ants. Our product are well-known for their exceptional quality and flavor. Moreover, our product are not only wholesomely healthy but also of constant quality and value for money, because we use only the freshest natural ingredients made using traditional techniques and thorough quality control. The importance of product and service quality also adds value to the customers. Furthermore, the pricing of Bilis Rangup items is reasonable and cost-effective for clients. Finally, our location is incredibly crucial because our company is located in a popular area
